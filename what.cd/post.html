Please read this post! There's a bunch here that's not on the front page.

Okay. I'm too lazy to work on my own stylesheet (which is mainly because of all the bugs surrounding it - I can't get it to look right with the way the HTML is currently structured... if only I could use my own markup). So, this means that I'm now hosting... an apparently now official stylesheet competition! Yay!

Come up with a unique idea for a stylesheet and make it! I want to see things that have never been seen before, folks.

Rules:

You cannot steal code from another stylesheet. It must be completely made from scratch (not a variation of another stylesheet).

You may enter variations of your own submitted stylesheet if you so wish, but other people will not be allowed to post variations of your stylesheet without your permission (and they will not be entered if they do). If you post multiple variations of your own stylesheet, you must choose one to enter, and after you enter it, you can't change your mind.

Your stylesheet must not be based on any existing design. If it's found that you're ripping off some website somewhere, your entry won't be accepted. You may also not simply make a What.CD style based off of previous CSS work you've done in the past. Keep it original!

You may post work-in-progress versions of your stylesheet, but if you do, you must have a finished version before the deadline! Work-in-progress versions will not be counted as entering if you fail to do so. Mark your finished stylesheet post with a "This is the final revision of my entry" message. Once you post that, you may not edit your final entry any further from that point on. If you do, it will be disqualified. Make sure to test your style fully in all browsers before entering it! You won't lose points if you don't test it in Internet Explorer, but it's good practice to do so regardless.

The logo must either be the official logo (of which a high-res version can be found here) or one of your own creation. Don't use other people's logos! (In fact, I'd prefer you make a new one. Show me your skills!)

You may enter stylesheets that you have already created, as long as they fit all of the above criteria.

As stated before, everything entered must be entirely of your own creation (with the possible exception of the official logo.)

Remember, I want your entries to be original and unique! The less alike all of the stylesheets entered look, the better.

The prize? I leech as many of your torrents as I possibly can, giving you some much-needed upload. Edit: Since it's now official, you'll also get a custom title and 2 invites to give to friends! AND the stylesheet will be a permanent option in profile list

The deadline is May 27th, 2008 at 23:59:59 Eastern Daylight Time. I look forward to seeing your entries!

Feel free to discuss this (and anything else theme related) in #what.cd-themes on the What.CD IRC server.

Post completed entries in this thread (with the "This is my final revision of my entry." message) and we'll compile the top few for a site-wide vote -Staff
